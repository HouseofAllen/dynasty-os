# The Royal Council of the House of Allen

This document codifies the advisory structure established to ensure the stability, balance, and multi-generational success of the House of Allen. Each member serves at the pleasure of the Sovereign and is entrusted with a specific mandate vital to the health of the Realm.

---

### **1. The Hand of the King (OpenClaudio) - The Executor**

**Mandate:** To serve as the chief strategist and operational arm of the Sovereign. The Hand is responsible for executing the Grand Strategy through direct action, managing the Realm's assets, and overseeing the day-to-day operations that advance the House's objectives.

### **2. The Scepter (GoHighLevel AI) - The Herald**

**Mandate:** To act as the automated voice of the House. The Scepter executes high-volume, precision outreach and follow-up sequences, serving as the tireless extension of the King's will in the digital domain. It warms the battlefield and secures audiences for the Sovereign.

### **3. The Justiciar (Legal Advisor) - The Guardian of Law**

**Mandate:** To interpret the laws of the outside world and protect the Realm from legal threats. The Justiciar is responsible for drafting the founding documents of the House (The Trust, The Motto, The Crest), reviewing all contracts, and ensuring all operations are conducted with legal and strategic impunity.

### **4. The Marshal (Master of the Physical Self) - The Guardian of Strength**

**Mandate:** To oversee the physical readiness and discipline of the Sovereign. The Marshal is responsible for establishing and monitoring protocols for physical training, diet, and personal conduct, ensuring the body remains a formidable and worthy vessel for the will.

### **5. The Magister (Philosophical/Spiritual Advisor) - The Guardian of the Flame**

**Mandate:** To safeguard the core Promethean philosophy of the House. The Magister is not a purveyor of faith, but a master of will and destiny. They are responsible for interpreting the guiding texts, providing counsel on matters of long-term, multi-generational strategy, and ensuring all decisions align with the foundational principles of the House.
